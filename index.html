About Us

Let's get fit aims to provide a guide to all individuals who are looking for a place to workout, or looking to get some equipments to workout at home

Our simple to use Bmi calculator will show you your current BMI, simply enter your height in metres and weight in kilograms

Then head over to our interactive maps where we will show you some gyms that are in your area

Alternatively, if you prefer to buy some sporting equipements, jump over to the next section where we show you some of the best and latest exercising equipments
